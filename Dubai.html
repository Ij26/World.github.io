Welcome to

Dubai

Dubai, the dazzling city of the United Arab Emirates, captivates with its striking blend of modern marvels and timeless traditions. Towering skyscrapers pierce the sky, including the iconic Burj Khalifa, a testament to human ingenuity and ambition. Amidst the gleaming architecture lies a vibrant tapestry of cultures, with bustling souks offering a sensory journey through the aromas and colors of the Middle East. Luxurious shopping malls beckon with designer boutiques and entertainment venues, while pristine beaches provide a serene escape. Dubai's allure extends beyond its urban landscape, with desert safaris offering a glimpse into the region's rich Bedouin heritage. As a global business hub, Dubai pulsates with energy, attracting entrepreneurs and multinational corporations alike. Yet amidst the bustling commerce, the city remains rooted in hospitality and warmth, welcoming visitors with open arms to experience its unique fusion of tradition and innovation

Places to Visit in Dubai

Burj Khalifa

Witness panoramic views of the Dubai Scape from the Observation Deck at the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world Travel all the way to 124th floor of the Burj Khalifa on the world's fastest glass elevator Enjoy a meal at the world’s highest restaurant, At.mosphere, located on the 122nd floor of the world’s highest building Marvel at the indoor fountain in the Burj Khalifa, which is lit up with 6,000 twinkling lights in the evening Unwind at the Burj Khalifa Lounge, which prides itself as the highest lounge in the world To add to your experience here, catch unobstructed views of the Dubai Fountain show from the Observation Deck on the 124th floor Location: It is located at 1 Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Blvd -

Dubai Price: 132 - 303 AED

Timings:
For 124th & 125th Floor:- The time slots for Non-prime hours are from 10:00 am to 3:30 pm and 07:00 pm to 11:00 pm and the time slots for Prime hours are 4:00 pm to 6.30 pm.
For 148th,124th & 125th Floor:- The time slots for Non-prime hours are 07:00 pm, 08:00 pm, 9:00 pm, and the time slots for Prime hours are 12:00 pm to 6.00 pm.

Palm Jumeirah

Admire the architecture of the Palm Jumeirah islands which are, quite literally, shaped like a palm tree and are amongst the must visit tourist places in Dubai At Aquaventure Waterpark, you may unleash your inner child by riding record-breaking waterslides, splashing in pools, or relaxing on the private beach with a cocktail. Visit The Lost Chambers Aquarium, where you may snorkel or dive through lagoons filled with 65,000 aquatic creatures, including 14 shark and ray species. Take a boat tour around the Palm Jumeirah coast, catching sight of some of the most iconic buildings here. Freefall along the Palm in a tandem skydiving session with Skydive Dubai, an experience tailored especially for adventure , Palm Jumeirah is not just an architectural marvel; it's a symbol of luxury and opulence. Sprinkled across its palm fronds are lavish villas, upscale apartments, and world-class resorts, including the iconic Atlantis, The Palm.

Location: Dubai - United Arab Emirates

Price: 1516 - 1815 AED

Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner

Embark on an exhilarating journey into the heart of the Arabian Desert with a desert safari experience in Dubai, where adventure and enchantment await. As the sun dips below the horizon, the desert landscape transforms into a mesmerizing canvas of golden hues, setting the stage for an unforgettable evening. Climb aboard a rugged 4x4 vehicle and feel the adrenaline rush as your expert driver navigates the towering sand dunes, offering heart-pounding thrills and panoramic vistas. Along the way, immerse yourself in the timeless beauty of the desert, marveling at the vast expanse of pristine sand dunes stretching as far as the eye can see. As dusk falls, arrive at a traditional Bedouin-style camp nestled amidst the dunes, where a magical evening awaits. Indulge in a sumptuous BBQ dinner under the starlit sky, savoring a delectable array of grilled meats, aromatic kebabs, and traditional Middle Eastern delicacies. Enthralling entertainment awaits as you witness mesmerizing performances such as belly dancing, tanoura shows, and captivating fire dances, adding to the enchantment.

Dubai Aquarium

The Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo offer an enchanting journey into the mesmerizing world beneath the ocean's surface, right in the heart of the bustling city of Dubai. Situated within the Dubai Mall, this sprawling aquatic wonderland is a haven for marine enthusiasts and curious visitors alike. Step into the mesmerizing tunnel of the aquarium, where a kaleidoscope of colorful fish, graceful sharks, and majestic rays glide effortlessly overhead, providing an immersive experience that transports you to the depths of the ocean. The aquarium's mesmerizing centerpiece, the colossal acrylic viewing panel, offers an awe-inspiring glimpse into the bustling underwater ecosystem, showcasing thousands of marine creatures in their natural habitat. Adjacent to the aquarium lies the Underwater Zoo, where a fascinating array of aquatic species awaits, including playful otters, towering crocodiles, and elusive pirahnas.

Location: 3rd Floor, Dubai Mall, Financial Center Street, Along Sheik Zayed Road, Next to Burj Khalifa - Dubai - United Arab Emirates.

Price: 70 - 89 AED

Timings:
Sunday to Wednesday- 10AM to 10PM
Thursday to Saturday - 10AM to 12AM

Dubai Mall

The Dubai Mall stands as an epitome of grandeur and extravagance, nestled in the heart of the vibrant cityscape of Dubai. As one of the world's largest shopping destinations, it offers an unparalleled shopping experience that transcends mere retail therapy. Spanning over 12 million square feet, the mall boasts an extensive array of over 1,300 retail outlets, featuring international luxury brands, high-end fashion boutiques, and eclectic designer labels, ensuring that every shopper's desires are catered to. Yet, the Dubai Mall is not merely a shopping haven; it is a multifaceted leisure destination, offering a myriad of attractions and entertainment options to captivate visitors of all ages. From the mesmerizing Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo to the adrenaline-pumping thrills of the VR Park and the enchanting Dubai Fountain, the mall is a treasure trove of experiences waiting to be discovered. Moreover, its diverse dining options, ranging from gourmet restaurants to casual eateries, promise culinary delights to tantalize every palate. Whether you're indulging in a shopping spree, embarking on an adventure-filled day with family and friends, or simply soaking in the cosmopolitan ambiance, a visit to the Dubai Mall is an immersive journey into the soul of Dubai's vibrant culture and luxury lifestyle.

Global Village

Global Village in Dubai stands as a testament to the city's commitment to celebrating diversity and fostering cultural exchange on a global scale. This sprawling open-air cultural and entertainment destination brings together the rich tapestry of cultures from around the world in a vibrant and dynamic setting. Spanning over 1.6 million square feet, Global Village offers visitors an immersive journey across continents, with pavilions representing more than 75 countries. Each pavilion showcases the unique traditions, cuisines, crafts, and performances of its respective nation, providing an unparalleled opportunity for cultural discovery and exploration. From shopping for artisanal handicrafts and sampling exotic delicacies to enjoying live music, dance performances, and theatrical shows, Global Village offers a kaleidoscope of experiences that captivate the senses and ignite the spirit of wanderlust.

Location: Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road Dubai

Price: 13 - 16 AED

Best Time: Saturday-Wednesday: 04:00 pm - 12:00 am & Thursday-Friday: 04:00 pm - 01:00 am
